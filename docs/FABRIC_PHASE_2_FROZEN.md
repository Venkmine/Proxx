# Fabric Phase-2: Frozen

**Status:** COMPLETE AND FROZEN  
**Effective Date:** 29 December 2025

---

## Policy Statement

Fabric Phase-2 is complete and frozen as of 29 December 2025. No further modifications, extensions, or additions shall be made to Phase-2 scope.

---

## Explicit Scope

The following capabilities constitute the complete and final scope of Fabric Phase-2:

- Ingestion
- Persistence
- Intelligence
- Reports
- Export
- Snapshot
- Diff

---

## Operational Constraints

Fabric is strictly observational and read-only.

Fabric SHALL NOT:

- Initiate execution
- Initiate retries
- Generate alerts
- Produce recommendations
- Perform scoring
- Perform ranking
- Make policy decisions

---

## Phase Boundary

Any behavior beyond reporting and comparison requires Phase-3 authorization and implementation.

---

## Enforcement

This policy is binding. Deviations require formal Phase-3 approval.
